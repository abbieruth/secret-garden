The Garden of Prayer

Welcome to my Garden of Prayer, a place where you can find rest, comfort, hope, and peace. This is meant to be a place where we can learn and grow. Here I post blog articles, poems, songs, verses, and quotes that are meant to bring hope to the weary and help us grow. Here, you can read poems, songs, and verses under the weeping willow by the river, explore blog posts in the rose garden, or follow links through the forest from wherever you are. I will ask others to write out an article every now and again to add some variety to the garden. Whenever you are stressed, tired, or downhearted, know my gate is always open and the flowers are always in bloom. Come to the garden of prayer and find your rest in the Saviour. It is really His garden after all. I am simply the keeper of it. If you need prayer, someone to talk to, or have a special request concerning the website, you will find a mailbox at the bottom of each page. Until then, I'll be watering the lilies. They are doing so well this year!
