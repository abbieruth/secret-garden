By the Willow Tree

I have always loved weeping willows, ever since I was very young. They always looked so beautiful and peaceful. I think it would be a wonderful thing to sit under a willow tree by a river and read my Bible or a book of poems. But, as I have neither a willow to sit under nor a stream to read by, I made this page. It is my peaceful place where I can put all my favorite poems, songs, and Scripture verses. I hope you enjoy it as much as I do. Feel free to browse through my collection while you're here!
